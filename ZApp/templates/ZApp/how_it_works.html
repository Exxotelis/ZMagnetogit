How It Works

Welcome to Sticker Magnet! Follow these simple steps to get your custom stickers and designs up and running.

Step 1: Choose Your Design

Start by selecting the design you want to upload. You can either create a new design or choose an existing one to upload as a sticker. Make sure your image meets our format requirements (JPG, PNG, 300dpi).

Step 2: Upload Your Design

Head to the Upload Design page and follow the instructions to upload your design. You can also upload an image directly on our Upload Image page if you’re working with photos.

Step 3: View and Manage Designs

Once uploaded, you can view all your designs on the View Designs page. Here, you can edit, manage, or delete designs as needed.

Step 4: Place Your Order

After you’re happy with your designs, place your order. We’ll take care of the printing and shipping to get your stickers delivered to you as soon as possible!

Step 5: Receive and Enjoy!

Once your order arrives, you’ll have your custom stickers ready to go. Stick them, share them, or give them as gifts—the possibilities are endless!

Need Help?

If you need assistance at any step, don’t hesitate to reach out on our Contact Us page. We’re here to help!
