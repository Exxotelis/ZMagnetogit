Shipping & Delivery.

At ZMagneto, we strive to offer fast and reliable shipping. Here is information on our shipping and delivery policies:

Domestic Shipping

We offer domestic shipping with an estimated delivery time of 3-5 business days. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

International Shipping

International orders typically take 7-14 business days for delivery. Please note that customs fees may apply.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number to monitor your delivery.

Delivery Issues

If you encounter any issues with your delivery, please contact our support team, and we will assist you promptly.
